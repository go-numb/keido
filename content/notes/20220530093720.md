### liに対する unique key {#liに対する-unique-key}

Reactの仕様でul/liのなかではuniqueなidを振る必要がある.

> Warning: Every element in a seq should have a unique :key

```clojure
(defn lister [items]
  [:ul
   (for [item items]
     ^{:key item} [:li "Item " item])])
```
